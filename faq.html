Questions

Everything You Need To Know

How do I schedule a visit?

Open a property and click the Book Visit button. You can also request an agent callback.

Are listings verified?

Yes, property details, images, amenities, and availability are reviewed before listing.

Can I get home loan help?

Yes, our team can guide you with eligibility, documents, EMI planning, and partner banks.

Can I sell my property?

Yes, create an account and submit property details. Our team helps with pricing and listing.

Is dashboard free?

Yes, basic buyer dashboard access is free for saved homes, visits, and messages.

Do agents contact me?

Yes, verified agents can help with visits, property questions, and negotiation support.

Can I compare properties?

Yes, shortlist homes and compare pricing, location, amenities, and investment value.

Do you support rentals?

Yes, you can search rentals, schedule visits, and get agreement guidance.

How can I contact support?

Use the contact page form, request callback, or email support@stacklyestate.com.
